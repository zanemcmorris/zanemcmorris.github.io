Life as an Engineer

I am an electrical computer engineering student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I am in the Bachelor Accelerated Masters program in which I am studying embedded software.

I have been interesting in engineering for the majority of my life. In high school, I was captain of the robotics team. During my freshman year, I worked on a team for a projects class where I learned I am a natural leader. I decided to become a TA for several of my favorite classes such as Digital Logic, RTOS, and Embedded Software Engineering, and most recently the senior design course. In the summer of 2023, I worked at Seagate Technology as a Servo Product Development intern. I thoroughly enjoyed this role and I returned for the summer of 2024. After my time at Seagate I had an industry connection to Western Digital where I had a firmware engineering internship. Most recently I had an internship at Micron as a firmware engineer for SSDs instead of harddrive systems like I had been doing for the previous few years. Now I am starting my full-time job search.
